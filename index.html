WHO WE ARE

Grill is a made-from-scratch Operation that prides itself on up-scale takes on regular "Bar Fare". We smoke our own meats, hand-bread all of our breaded items, and 90% of our recipes are made in-house through hard work and attention to detail. Come and Taste the Difference!

WE OFFER CATERING

Big or Small, We Cater it All! We've done small in-house caterings, and weddings as large as 350 people! We have options available but can also bring your ideas to life and go off menu to cater to your needs! Please send us a requeston our "contact" page for more information!

PRIVATE PARTIES

Both of our restaurants have dedicated party rooms and can accomodate up to 60 people! We can also cater these private on-site parties to create your ideal experience for you and your people! Please send us a request through our "Contact Page" for more information!
